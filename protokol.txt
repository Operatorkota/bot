ORDERLY PROTOCOL

This is a guide for orderly

Patient Classification
Forensics:
R1 Detection of contraband, through a scan or by visual
R2 Continuing to be aggressive after being in isolation
R4 Escape Attempts, or affiliating with an escape plot
Max Security:
R1 Proven murder of another patient or staff (Either by visual or by logic eg: 1 locked room, 1 dead patient with blood marks and 1 alive patient in the same room)
R2 Attempted murder of a staff member 
R3 Threatening with a visible edged weapon
R4 Raiding the facility with firearms
R5 Nearly Escaping/Already outside

Guidelines for Tools:

Handcuffs:
A temporary way to minimize interruptance from other patients as you deal with something, very important for this
A temporary way to prevent a patient from using contraband or being aggressive
When enforcing them to isolation/forensics, you may cuff them to restrict them from gathering contraband or to restrict violent behaviour


Taser
Note that the taser shoots to your mouse and is physics based
Usages:
When a patient is attempting to escape detainment
If a patient is attempting to attack you
Put people at taserpoint, soft fearRP
In SRP, if you have a mic, say “Taser taser taser” when deploying the taser


Riot Shield
This is picked up from the armory, unless you are enforcement orderly+
Left click to hit with the shield, which stuns for a brief moment

Usages: 
Close quarter situations, where there's high risk for you due to low distancing
When going against a group of patients that are aggressive
Stunning in order to cuff without them running away (in group situations)

 Metal Detector:
When you suspect a patient may have contraband, you may check them with this tool
Random checking is allowed

Stun gun (Enforcement Orderly+):
Use when getting choked, it will ragdoll the person choking you
Another way to stun a patient

Orderly Duty:
Provide medication, food and water to all types of patients
Monitor patients and preserve their wellbeing
Enforce punishments on patients
Maintain a safe environment for patients and staff
Control patients positions based on schedule
Isolate unarmed staff from patients to minimize risk
Show authority to patients as to not get overpowered by them
